Hello - It's You

created by mitraavrs

a semi-autobiographical hypertext combining text and image in an exploration of memory and the journey to finding the self.

Instructions: Navigate by clicking hyperlinks and image-maps. Some sections have text that appear on cursor hover. Best viewed on desktop and best experienced with headphones.
The most important instruction - have fun, explore. Lose yourself in the hyperlinks.

Dedicated to the people I love, and the people who love me tenfold. Your sincerity scares me.

PHOTOSENSITIVITY WARNING
This hypertext contains flashing images and patterns that may trigger seizures in people with photosensitive epilepsy or other photosensitivities. Even people who have no history of seizures or epilepsy may have an undiagnosed condition that can cause these photosensitive epileptic seizures. Viewer discretion is advised.
